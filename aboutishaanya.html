About Ishaanya

Ishaanya first started in the year 2011. The name “Ishaanya” is derived from the Bengali word “Ishaan cone” which means “Northeast”. According to Hindu mythology “Ishaan cone” is the most sacred and hence the name “Ishaanya”

Ishaanya is the Annual Tech Cultural Fest of “Tripura Institute of Technology”,Narsingarh, Agartala. The event sports technical as well as cultural events. The mega musical night is what brings craze to the fest.
